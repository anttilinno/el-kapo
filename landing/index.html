Two you remember · two you're guessing

You play half-blind

At the deal, you get to peek at just two of your four face-down cards. Then they flip back over.

From there it's all memory and read. Every draw is a gamble on what you're still holding, every opponent's move a tell. The lowest hand total wins the round — a Joker is worth nothing, a King stings for thirteen. The trick is knowing when your two secrets have stayed low enough to bet on.

A turn, start to finish

Four beats repeat around the table until someone dares to end the round.

01 · Peek

Learn two

At the deal, memorize two of your four cards. Hold them in your head — they won't turn face-up again.

02 · Draw

Take a card

Draw the top of the deck or lift the face-up discard. Swap it into your hand, or throw it away.

03 · Power

Trigger a card

Discarding a 7 through Queen unlocks its power — spy on a card, or blind-swap one with a rival.

04 · Kapo

Call it

Think you're lowest? Call Kapo. Everyone gets one last turn, hands flip, and the smallest total wins.
